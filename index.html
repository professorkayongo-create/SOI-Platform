What is Source of Information?

Source of Information, commonly known as SOI, is an educational platform that promotes access to knowledge and encourages students to explore science, technology, mathematics, innovation, research, and practical problem-solving. SOI aims to empower learners with information and skills that can help them succeed academically and contribute positively to their communities.
