Meet the SOI Committee

The SOI Committee supports the development of the platform by coordinating educational activities, science and technology projects, student participation, innovation, and access to learning resources.

Committee Leadership

Chairperson

Committee Leader

Provides overall leadership and direction for the SOI platform and coordinates major activities and initiatives.

Vice Chairperson

Deputy Committee Leader

Supports the Chairperson and helps coordinate committee activities and projects.

Secretary

Administration & Records

Maintains committee records, prepares meeting minutes, manages communication, and organizes important documents.

Treasurer

Finance & Resources

Manages financial records, budgets, fundraising activities, and responsible use of project resources.

ICT & Technology Officer

Digital Platform Management

Supports website development, digital tools, technology projects, and technical operations of the SOI platform.

Science Coordinator

Science Education & Projects

Coordinates science learning activities, experiments, academic resources, and science projects.

Innovation Officer

Innovation & Research

Encourages creative problem-solving, research, student inventions, and innovative solutions to community challenges.

Communication Officer

Public Relations & Outreach

Manages communication, announcements, outreach, and information sharing with students and the wider community.

General Committee Member

Student Support & Participation

Participates in committee activities and supports the implementation of SOI programs and projects.

Committee Responsibilities

1. Leadership and Coordination

Provide effective leadership and coordinate the activities and development of the SOI platform.

2. Academic Support

Promote access to quality learning materials and support students in Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics, Physics, and other academic areas.

3. Science and Technology

Encourage students to participate in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics activities.

4. Innovation and Research

Support students in developing innovative ideas, conducting research, and creating solutions to real-world problems.

5. Digital Platform Management

Maintain and improve the SOI digital platform and promote responsible use of technology.

6. Community Engagement

Connect students and communities through educational activities, outreach programs, and information sharing.

7. Project Development

Identify, develop, and support practical projects that contribute to education, technology, environmental sustainability, and community development.

If you have an idea, project, question, or would like to participate in SOI activities, contact the committee through the contact page.
